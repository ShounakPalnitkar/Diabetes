Security Information

All data in the Diabetes Management Suite is protected with:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest and in transit
  • Secure key exchange using RSA-OAEP
  • Application sandboxing to prevent cross-site attacks
  • Client-side encryption before data transmission

Your health data is never stored or transmitted in plaintext.
